Substitute Studs 2023

Studs is a project at the Computer Science chapter where we aim to provide a platform for companies to engage with soon-to-be-graduated students through exclusive events. In case a member of the Studs project group cannot attend an event, there is an opportunity to attend as a substitute.

The events are typically held at the office of the company. They are ~3h, food will be served, and you will get the chance to mingle with company representatives.
